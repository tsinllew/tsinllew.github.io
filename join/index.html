    Join Us

    National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST) is one of the largest public research organizations in Japan, with 2,300 researchers and an annual research budget of approximately $1 billion. According to Nature Index 2022, AIST ranks 25th among government institutions worldwide. Artificial Intelligence Research Center (AIRC), as one of the three core centers of Japanese AI Strategy 2019, serves as a hub for achieving AI that boosts industrial competitiveness and achieves a more abundant society.

    We are looking for RA / interns, visiting Ph.D. students, and Ph.D. candidates, who are interested in pursuing Graph Analysis / Machine Learning / AI related research. Research topics include but are not limited to

      • Social network dynamics

    • Graph Data Management

      • Graph visualization

    • Applications of Graph-Powered Machine Learning
